Pattern-generated shifts are created with OT treatment set to Never overtime — scheduled rotation shifts don't qualify for OT even when the daily/weekly rules would apply. Their hours still count toward the thresholds, though: pick up an extra shift on a pattern day (or in a pattern-heavy week) and the extra shift is automatically flagged as OT.
